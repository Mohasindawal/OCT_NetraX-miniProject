What is Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)?

OCT is a non-invasive medical imaging technology that provides high-resolution, cross-sectional images of biological tissues. It's widely used in ophthalmology, cardiology, and various other medical fields to diagnose and monitor conditions.

How does OCT benefit healthcare professionals?

OCT offers healthcare professionals detailed, real-time images of tissues, allowing for early disease detection, precise diagnostics, and treatment monitoring. It enhances decision-making and patient care.
